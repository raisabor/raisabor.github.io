[ About me ]

Hello, I am a fourth year undergraduate student studying Computer Science at Southern Methodist University's Bobby B. Lyle School of Engineering. My childhood was full of travel and I have lived all over the United States. I came to Dallas/Fort Worth at the start of high school and have lived here ever since. My initial exposure to computer science was in the 9th grade. I took Pre-AP Computer Science, built very simple programs in Scratch, took AP Computer Science the next semester and started coding in Java. Back then technology was just an interest but now I see all of the fulfilling careers I can pursue because of my skills. Right now, my favorite area in Computer Science is Robotics and I can't wait to see what the future holds.
